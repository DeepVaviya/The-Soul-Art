FREQUENTLY ASKED

How far in advance should I book?

For bridal mehendi, we recommend booking 2-3 months in advance, especially during wedding season.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes! We love destination weddings. Travel charges apply based on location.

What type of henna do you use?

We use 100% natural, organic henna mixed fresh for each session, free from chemicals and PPD.

How long does the mehendi last?

With proper aftercare, bridal mehendi typically lasts 1-3 weeks, darkening to a rich brown within 24-48 hours.
